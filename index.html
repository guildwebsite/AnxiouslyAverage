About Us

Hello, we are Anxiously Average and we are a casual raiding guild for World of Warcraft: The Burning Cursade on Mankirk. We are just a group of friends that met in Classic and decided to make a guild. We are cultivating a chill enviorment that is drama free and fun where experienced and new players can enjoy the game together. At the start our main priority will be to get the raid group up and running but we will work towards a active guild that is upbeat and social. We just ask that you treat everyone with respect, don't bring up politics, and be friendly and willing to help out a fellow guidlie. If you have any questions please let us know!

Anyone is welcome to join the guild! We are currently only running one raid group. However, the second group will be up and running in the near future.


Raid Information

Server: Mankirk

Type: Semi-Hardcore/Casual

Loot System: MS>OS gear reservation which is completed futher down the page. Non-reserved gear will be +1 system. Raid leaders will have a final say so there is even distribution of gear.

Raid Times: Friday and Saturday at 8pm to 11pm server time BOTH teams will be running at this time.

Required Addons: Deadly Boss Mod (DBM), Threat Metre (Tiny Threat - Details / other threat addon)

Requirements to Raid: We trust the you know your class and to know when your ready. We expect you to have atleast a few pre-raid BIS and actively working on gear between runs. We ask you come prepared with food buffs, and whatever else you feel is neccesary to complete the run.
